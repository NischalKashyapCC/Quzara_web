Quzara

Quzara, LLC is a Cyber Security firm located within the technical corridor of the DC metropolitan area in Reston, Virginia. The company was founded by Qudsia Malik, a board-certified physician, in 2015. The founders and CEO of Quzara have over twenty (20) years of experience in major healthcare and consulting firms focused on Cyber Security. As a Minority-Owned and Woman-Owned Small Business, Quzara, brings a diverse skilled team to the market. Initially, Quzara did Cyber Security consulting for commercial client. Later, Quzara started to provide Cyber Security Solutions such as Application Security Services, Risk Assessment Services, Cloud Security Services with Azure and AWS, HIPAA Compliance Services, FedRAMP Assessment Services, Security Architecture Services and FISMA Support Services. These services are what we currently provide to commercial customers and in some limited capacity as a subcontractor to contractors in the federal market.

Today, Quzara’s team of architecture and compliance experts guide customers to achieve their specific Cyber Security needs. A strong, customer-focused approach, and the constant quest for top-class quality have enabled Quzara to strive in the Cyber Security field. Additionally, Quzara is committed to its worldwide network of partners including Carahsoft, Splunk, AWS, Veracode and Rapid7. Our strategic alliances and reseller capabilities bring innovation and improved offerings to our customers.

Our team maintains and continues to develop awareness of the field by participating in innovative platforms given by membership workshops in the Washington D.C. area dedicated to cutting edge Cyber Security issues, such as the Armed Forces Communications and Electronics Association, the American Council for Technology, the Open Web Application Security Project, the Northern Virginia Technology Council, the Cloud Security Alliance , the Information Systems Security Association, and the Information Systems Audit and Control Association.
