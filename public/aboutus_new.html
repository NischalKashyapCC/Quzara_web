Who We Are

Quzara, LLC is a Cyber Security firm located within the technical corridor of the DC metropolitan area in Reston, Virginia. The company was founded by Qudsia Malik, a board-certified physician, in 2015. The founders and CEO of Quzara have over twenty (20) years of experience in major healthcare and consulting firms focused on Cyber Security.
